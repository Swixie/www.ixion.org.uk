Ixion Events Calendar

What's going on 'ere, then?

Well, to put it simply, the subscribers to the Ixion motorcycle mailing list have got such hectic lives, and organise so much stuff, that everyone was having trouble keeping track of WTF was supposed to be happening, and when.
That may all change now that there is a resource at which all and sundry may point their browsers and get the low down on the high life of the list.

New, improved calendar.


The ixion calendar is a live online thing, so you'll be able to see your entry immediately.
Take care because there is no facility for an individual to delete an incorrect entry. If you get it wrong, mail the calendarmaster who will sort it out.
If you need to put a link into the calendar, write the link using HTML tags in your entry.

How do I get an event listed?

At the bottom of each month is a form for you to add your entry.
