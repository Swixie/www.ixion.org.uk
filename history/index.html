Ixion - History

Ixion was originally set up by Dave Edmondson on 27th July 1990 at Queen Mary and Westfield College in East London. Malcolm Jackson at University of Nottingham took over the running in June 1993, until on 29th January 2002 Ixion left Nottingham to live on quacky, a server owned by ixies.

Originally called Ogri (after the Ogri character in Bike magazine, the name was changed to Ixion on 16th May 1996, after a complaint by Paul Sample.
