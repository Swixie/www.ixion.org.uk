Jeff Wain (1942-2001)

It is with deep regret that we mourn the passing of Jeff Wain.

After an accident in Norway, Jeff lost his struggle for life on Monday 2 July 2001.

He was a much loved friend to all of us. His kindness, generosity, and good humour will be missed.

Jeff's funeral was held on Monday 9 July 2000 at 11:30 in the Church of the Ascension, Whixley, North Yorkshire.
